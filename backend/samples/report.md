# Q3 FY2024 Equity Research — Acme Robotics (ACME)

Acme Robotics reported total revenue of $1,250 million in Q3 FY2024, up from
$1,000 million in Q3 FY2023. This represents year-over-year revenue growth of 25%.

Gross profit for the quarter was $500 million, implying a gross margin of 40%.
Operating expenses totaled $300 million, leaving operating income of $200 million.

The company's cloud segment grew faster than its hardware segment, with cloud revenue
of $450 million compared to hardware revenue of $800 million. Management attributed the
strong cloud growth to increased enterprise adoption, because new multi-year contracts
drove recurring revenue higher.

Net income reached $210 million, a figure that exceeds operating income due to a
one-time tax benefit. Diluted earnings per share were $1.05 on 200 million shares.

We believe Acme is well positioned for long-term value creation. The company expects
revenue to reach $5,500 million for full-year FY2025.
